About Me


Intro

Hi, I am Kyri Lea. I recently graduated from Rochester Institute of Technology with a BS/MS degree in Cybersecurity. I love mainframes and z/OS, and I have been working on creating more open-source tooling for mainframe pentesting as part of my most recent internship and co-op with IBM X-Force Red. I also enjoy data privacy and all things Linux. Throughout college I participated in a number of Red vs. Blue, pentesting, and CTF competitions, something I hope to continue to be involved with in the future.

I have six summers of experience working in the cybersecurity field. I spent four summers at Digi-Key Electronics working with the security team on data privacy, application security, logging and monitoring systems, and identity and access management. For the past two summers, I interned on IBM's X-Force Red where I researched security vulnerabilities associated with smart gym equipment and developed M-Ray, a z/OS pentesting tool.
