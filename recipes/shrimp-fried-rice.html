Shrimp Fried Rice


Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 lb peeled, devined, medium shrimp, thawed
  • Salt
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 1 1/2 cups frozen mixed vegetables
  • 6 cups cooked rice, cold
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 4 eggs
  • Yum Yum sauce

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat a large skillet on high. Add olive oil, shrimp, salt, garlic powder. Cook 2-4 minutes until shrimp starts to turn golden, stirring occasionally. Transfer shrimp to a plate.
  2. Add vegetables and onion to the oil and cook until the vegetables are mostly thawed, 1-2 minutes.
  3. Add rice and soy sauce, cook 5-7 minutes until rice starts to become crispy.
  4. Move rice to the side of the pan, lower heat to medium. Add butter to empty side of pan.
  5. Crack eggs into butter, stir until scrambled. Stir scrambled eggs into rice.
  6. Add shrimp to pan and stir in
  7. Let sit for a few minutes, then stir in Yum Yum sauce.
