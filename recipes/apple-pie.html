Apple Pie


Ingredients

  • Refrigerated pie crust
  • 3/4 lb Granny Smith apples
  • 3/4 lb Golden Delicious apples
  • 1 1/2 tbsp sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp flour
  • 1/8 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 egg

Cooking Instructions

  1. Roll pie crust to 11". Place on baking sheet.
  2. Cut apples into 1/2 inch wedges.
  3. Toss apples with sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt.
  4. Place apples on crust, leaving a 2 inch border. Fold the edges of the crust over the apples.
  5. Brush beaten egg over the crust.
  6. Bake at 375 degrees F for 1 hour.
