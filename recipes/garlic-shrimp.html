Garlic Shrimp


Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • 1/2 cup broth
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp crushed red pepper
  • black pepper
  • 1 3/4 lbs large shrimp, shelled
  • 1/3 cup parsley, chopped
  • fresh lemon juice from 1/2 lemon
  • pasta or bread for serving

Cooking Instructions

  1. Melt butter and olive oil in a skillet. Saute garlic for 1 minute.
  2. Add broth, salt, red pepper, and black pepper. Simmer for 2 minutes.
  3. Add shrimp and saute until they turn pink, 2-4 minutes.
  4. Add parsley and lemon juice. Serve over pasta or with bread.
