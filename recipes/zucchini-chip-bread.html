Zucchini Chip Bread


Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 3 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground allspice
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 tsp vanilla
  • 2 cups grated zucchini
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine flour, sugar, cinnamon, soda, powder, and allspice in a large bowl.
  2. Beat eggs, oil, and vanilla in a small bowl. Combine with dry ingredients until moistened.
  3. Fold in zucchini and chocolate chips.
  4. Transfer to two greased 8"x4"x2" loaf pans.
  5. Bake at 325 degrees F for 60-75 minutes.
