Sausage and Brussels Sprouts Ravioli


Ingredients

  • 1 pound cheese ravioli
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 pound Italian turkey sausage, casing removed
  • 12 oz brussels sprouts, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • Grated parmesan, for serving
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cook ravioli according to package.
  2. Heat oil in large skillet on medium high.
  3. Add sausage, and break apart. Cook 6-7 minutes, until browned.
  4. Add brussels sprouts, salt, and pepper. Cook 2 minutes.
  5. Add broth and bring to simmer.
  6. Serve over ravioli and sprinkle with cheese.
