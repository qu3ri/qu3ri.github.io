Kiwi Fish Tacos


Ingredients

  • 2 kiwis, peeled and diced
  • 1 avocado, diced
  • 2 tbsp pickled jalapenos
  • 2 tbsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1 tbsp lime juice
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 10-oz package frozen fish sticks
  • 8 small corn tortillas
  • Sour cream

Cooking Instructions

  1. Combine kiwis, avocado, jalapenos, cilantro, lime juice, and salt in a bowl to make salsa.
  2. Prepare fish sticks according to package.
  3. Fill tortillas with fish sticks and salsa, top with sour cream.
